Most of these men are Dancing Men, per the Sherlock Holmes story of the same name. Using a Dancing Men codesheet (and ignoring the men with two flags), you can read the message:

TWICE FLAGGED MEN HOLD VIGENERES KEY UHO AFVOW YCBIIT YOD ZVFJTXQIE YOIK BNCAVV

"Naval" and men with two flags both point to semaphore. Reading the two-flagged men as semaphore, the key is
BAKER ELEMENTARY

The remainder of the message then decodes to:
THE WORDS MYOPIC AND PROFITEER FORM ANSWER

MYOPIC PROFITEER
